Why now

A gap nobody
has filled yet.

Strategy games generate billions a year. None of them are tethered to reality. Tycoon.live changes that.

01

Real-world data as a moat

Income in the game is calculated from live infrastructure data: EV charging sessions, wind farm output, match schedules, flight operations. This creates a defensible data layer that gets harder to replicate over time, not easier.

02

70 million assets already mapped

The content already exists. We are not building fictional worlds. Every wind turbine, metro station, stadium and EV charger in the game is a real object at real GPS coordinates. The map scales to hundreds of millions of assets with no additional content cost.

03

Multiple monetisation layers

Asset sales, lootboxes, guild creation fees, marketplace transaction cuts, seasonal passes and a premium asset tier that unlocks through consortiums. None of these require pay-to-win mechanics because skill and timing are the actual differentiators.

04

Community as infrastructure

Guilds, consortiums and territorial control are built into the core loop. Players recruit other players. The social graph grows with the map. Retention comes from competition, not from artificial engagement hooks.
