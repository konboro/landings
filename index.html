How it works

Real world.
Real economy.

Every income tick is calculated from live APIs. Energy outputs, flight departures, match schedules. Nothing is made up.

1

Browse the map

A live world map built from real infrastructure data. Every marker sits at actual GPS coordinates and represents something you can own.

2

Build your portfolio

Buy directly, win auctions, open contracts. Each asset class comes with its own rarity and income ceiling. Some you find easily, some you hunt for weeks.

3

Reality drives income

A wind farm earns more when it's actually windy. A stadium pays out on real match days. Your income follows the real world whether you're watching or not.

4

Dominate the rankings

Trade on the open market and climb the global leaderboard. The best assets are scarce and people fight over them. Getting there first matters.

5

Form consortiums

Some assets are too big to buy alone. Pool resources with other players, manage a shared treasury and divide the profits. Corporate play opens up a completely different tier of the game.

6

Customize your company

Get unique skins and build a fleet that stands out. Make your presence visible on the map with visual upgrades and rare cosmetic collections.

7

Location gives you an edge

Set up a branch close to the infrastructure you own and collect proximity bonuses on top of base income. GPS is not just decoration here.

8

Deep stats, your own path

Every asset tracks utilisation rates, peak hours, weather correlation and ROI over time. Read the data, spot what others miss, and build a strategy nobody else is running.
